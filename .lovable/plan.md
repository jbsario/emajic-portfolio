
# Ethereal Majic — Company Portfolio Website

## Visual Style
- **Dark & luxurious** theme: Deep charcoal/near-black backgrounds with warm gold accents
- Elegant typography with a modern serif for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- Warm lighting aesthetic matching interior design imagery

## Sections (Single-Page Layout)

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-screen hero with a stunning interior design background image
- Company name "ETHEREAL MAJIC" with tagline "Interior Design"
- Smooth scroll-down indicator
- Sticky transparent navbar with smooth section navigation (Home, About, Services, Projects, Contact)

### 2. About Section
- Company introduction with fade-in scroll animations
- Brief story about the company's vision in design, renovation, and architecture
- Stats counters (years of experience, projects completed, happy clients)

### 3. Services Section
- Three service cards with hover effects:
  - **Interior Design** — Modern residential and commercial interiors
  - **Renovation** — Full-scale renovation and remodeling
  - **Glass & Aluminum Works** — Custom glass and aluminum installations
- Each card animates in on scroll with icon and description

### 4. Project Gallery
- Filterable grid (All / Interior Design / Renovation / Glass & Aluminum)
- Uses the Behance project images as showcase pieces
- Hover overlay with project name and category
- Click to open **image lightbox** for fullscreen viewing
- Masonry-style or uniform grid layout with staggered scroll animations

### 5. Contact Section
- Contact form (Name, Email, Phone, Message)
- Company contact info and social links
- Dark card with gold accent border

### 6. Footer
- Company logo, copyright, quick nav links

## Interactive Features
- **Scroll animations**: Elements fade/slide in as they enter the viewport (using Intersection Observer)
- **Image lightbox**: Fullscreen overlay when clicking gallery images with navigation
- **Smooth navigation**: Navbar links scroll smoothly to each section; navbar becomes solid on scroll
- **Hover effects**: Gallery cards scale and reveal overlay on hover; service cards lift with shadow
